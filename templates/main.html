Molecular Thesaurus

Welcome to the Molecular Thesaurus!

This website combines different open-source cheminformatics databases and toolkits (RDKit, ChEMBL, Ketcher, PubChem) to gather information on any entered molecule and help you find similar ones. The ChEMBL bioactive molecule database is used for finding similar molecules, so this website produces the best results when pharmacological molecules are entered. If you have any suggestions on what could be improved/added, please email me at jack.gdouglass@gmail.com!
